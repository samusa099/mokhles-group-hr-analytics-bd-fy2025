# HR Dashboard Blueprint

## Page 1 — Executive Overview

KPI cards for active headcount, hires, exits, turnover, monthly gross payroll and engagement; monthly headcount and hire/exit trends; department headcount and workforce composition.

## Page 2 — Workforce and Diversity

Department/location headcount, job levels, gender representation, age bands, education profile and disability-status representation.

## Page 3 — Recruitment

Recruitment funnel, requisition status, source effectiveness, time-to-fill, recruitment cost and candidate experience.

## Page 4 — Retention and Leave

Turnover trend, exit reasons, regrettable exits, tenure at exit, leave type, leave days by department and absence rate.

## Page 5 — Learning and Performance

Training hours, completion rate, assessment improvement, training cost, performance-rating distribution, potential and promotion readiness.

## Page 6 — Compensation and Safety

Gross pay by job level, annual total reward, compa-ratio, incidents by type/severity, lost-time injuries, days lost and corrective-action status.

## Common filters

- Department
- Location
- Job level
- Gender
- Employment type
- Date
