# Dataset Card

## Dataset name

Mokhles Group HR Analytics Demo 2025

## Summary

A realistic synthetic HR analytics portfolio designed around a Bangladesh
business environment. It includes employee, recruitment, separation, leave,
diversity, learning, compensation, performance and health-and-safety data.

## Intended uses

- HR analytics education
- Excel and Power BI portfolio development
- Python, SQL and Tableau practice
- Workforce-planning exercises
- Recruitment and turnover analysis
- Compensation and performance analysis

## Out-of-scope uses

- Making decisions about real employees
- Representing the records as actual company data
- Medical, legal or financial decision-making
- Training systems to infer protected characteristics about real individuals

## Sensitive attributes

The synthetic dataset includes gender, age and disability-status fields for
responsible diversity-analysis practice. It intentionally excludes religion,
political views and unnecessary identifiers.

## Limitations

The records are generated and do not capture every legal, cultural or
organisational nuance of employment in Bangladesh. Analytical findings should
be interpreted as portfolio examples rather than real business conclusions.
