# About the Dataset

This portfolio contains a realistic synthetic HR analytics dataset designed
around a Bangladesh-based business environment for FY2025.

It includes analysis-ready CSV tables, a consolidated Excel master
workbook, 12 specialised HR reports, an exploratory Python notebook,
structured metadata and complete documentation.

## Analytics coverage

- Employee master data and workforce structure
- Monthly, quarterly and annual HR KPIs
- Recruitment funnel and time-to-fill
- Employee turnover and separation analysis
- Leave and absenteeism
- Diversity and inclusion
- Training and development
- Compensation and benefits
- Performance management
- Workplace health and safety

All records are fully synthetic and were created exclusively for analytics
practice, portfolio development, education and demonstration purposes.
